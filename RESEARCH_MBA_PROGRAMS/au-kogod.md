# AU Kogod School of Business -- ITEC Graduate Course Inventory

## Program Overview

The Kogod School of Business at American University offers a graduate IT Management program within its MBA and MS programs. The ITEC department provides two concentrations:

- **IT Management** -- focused on enterprise technology leadership, governance, and strategy
- **Analytics & Data Science** -- focused on data-driven decision making, machine learning, and business intelligence

**Program structure**: Core required courses + concentration electives + capstone project.

## ITEC Graduate Courses (16 courses)

### Core / Required

| Course | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| ITEC-610 | Enterprise IT Fundamentals | Required foundation course covering the IT landscape, governance, infrastructure, and management frameworks. The Enterprise IT Fundamentals Primer supports this course. |

### IT Management Track

| Course | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| ITEC-611 | IT Strategy & Governance | Alignment of IT with business strategy; COBIT, ITIL, and governance frameworks; CIO/CTO roles. |
| ITEC-630 | Business Process Management | Process modeling, analysis, and improvement; BPM lifecycle; process mining fundamentals. |
| ITEC-635 | Enterprise Architecture | TOGAF, Zachman, and other EA frameworks; application portfolio management; technology roadmapping. |
| ITEC-640 | Project Management for IT | IT project planning, execution, and delivery; Agile and Waterfall methodologies; PMI framework. |
| ITEC-645 | IT Financial Management | IT budgeting, TCO analysis, cost allocation, chargeback models, ROI for technology investments. |
| ITEC-650 | Cloud Computing & Infrastructure | IaaS/PaaS/SaaS models; cloud migration strategy; data center operations; hybrid architectures. |
| ITEC-655 | Vendor & Outsourcing Management | IT procurement, SLA management, nearshoring/offshoring, vendor evaluation and governance. |

### Analytics & Data Science Track

| Course | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| ITEC-620 | Data Analytics I | Python and R fundamentals for business analytics; exploratory data analysis; statistical foundations. |
| ITEC-621 | Data Analytics II (Machine Learning) | Supervised and unsupervised learning; model selection; business applications of ML. |
| ITEC-625 | Database Management Systems | Relational databases, SQL, data modeling, normalization, NoSQL overview. |
| ITEC-660 | Data Visualization & Business Intelligence | Dashboard design, BI tools (Tableau, Power BI), storytelling with data, KPI frameworks. |

### Cross-Cutting / Advanced

| Course | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| ITEC-665 | Digital Transformation Strategy | Organizational change through digital technologies; disruption frameworks; transformation roadmaps. |
| ITEC-668 | Cybersecurity Analytics | Threat landscape, risk quantification, security frameworks (NIST), privacy regulations. |
| ITEC-670 | AI for Business Leaders | AI strategy, generative AI, responsible AI, AI governance, business use cases. |

### Capstone

| Course | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| ITEC-680 | IT Capstone Project | Integrative project applying concepts from across the ITEC curriculum to a real-world client engagement. |

## Observations

- Strong coverage of traditional IT management topics (governance, EA, project management, vendor management).
- Solid analytics pipeline from fundamentals through machine learning.
- Cybersecurity and AI offerings reflect current market demand.
- Financial management of IT (ITEC-645) is a differentiator -- not commonly found at peer programs.
- The primer (supporting ITEC-610) serves as the entry point for all ITEC students and should therefore cover or introduce topics from across the full curriculum.
