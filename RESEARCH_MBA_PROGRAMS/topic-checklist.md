# Master Topic Checklist -- MBA IT/Analytics Curriculum Comparison

## Legend

**School coverage levels:**

- **CORE** = Required course or required module
- **ELECTIVE** = Dedicated elective course
- **COVERED** = Addressed within a broader course (not standalone)
- **--** = Not found in published catalog

**Primer status:**

- **STRONG** = Well-developed page with full template coverage
- **PARTIAL** = Page exists but incomplete or light treatment
- **GLOSSARY** = Term defined in glossary only; no dedicated page
- **MISSING** = Not currently addressed in the primer

**Gap flag:**

- **PRIORITY 1** = Covered at 4+ schools; missing or weak in primer; high relevance to ITEC-610
- **PRIORITY 2** = Covered at 3+ schools; missing or weak in primer; moderate relevance
- **PRIORITY 3** = Covered at 2+ schools; missing or weak in primer; niche or emerging
- **OK** = Adequate current coverage in primer
