# MBA Program IT/Analytics Curriculum Benchmarking Research

## Purpose

This research benchmarks the AU Kogod School of Business ITEC graduate curriculum against IT, analytics, and technology-related course offerings at five peer and aspirational MBA programs. The findings inform content priorities for the **Enterprise IT Fundamentals Primer** used in ITEC-610 and related courses.

## Methodology

- **Source material**: Publicly available course catalogs, program descriptions, concentration/track requirements, and faculty pages from each school's official website.
- **Scope**: Graduate-level (MBA and specialized master's) courses related to information technology, data analytics, digital transformation, and technology strategy.
- **Limitations**: Course catalogs reflect published offerings as of early 2026; not all listed courses are offered every term. Syllabus-level detail was not available for most programs.
