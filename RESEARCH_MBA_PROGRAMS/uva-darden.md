# UVA Darden School of Business -- AI & Data Analytics Course Inventory

## Program Overview

The Darden School of Business at the University of Virginia launched a **new AI & Data Analytics concentration in Fall 2025**, one of the most comprehensive technology-focused concentrations in MBA education. With 25+ courses, it reflects the urgency with which business schools are responding to employer demand for AI-literate MBA graduates.

Darden also houses the **LaCross Center for Ethical Leadership in Business**, which has expanded its focus to include AI ethics, responsible technology, and data governance -- positioning Darden as a leader in the ethics dimension of technology education.

## AI & Data Analytics Concentration (25+ courses)

## LaCross Center for Ethical Leadership -- AI Ethics Focus

The LaCross Center has developed specific programming around AI ethics:

- Faculty research on algorithmic fairness and accountability
- Student-led AI ethics case competition
- Executive education modules on responsible AI governance
- Partnership with industry on AI ethics standards development

## Observations

- Darden's 25+ course concentration is **the most expansive single technology concentration** among the programs surveyed.
- The **breadth of emerging technology coverage** (blockchain, quantum, edge computing, IoT) goes beyond what most MBA programs offer. These topics signal where business schools think the market is heading.
- The **AI ethics and responsible AI** courses, anchored by the LaCross Center, represent a structural investment in ethics that goes beyond a single course offering.
- **Software Design** as an MBA course is unusual and reflects a belief that business leaders need to understand how software is built, not just what it does.
- **Gap relative to AU Kogod**: Darden's emerging technology breadth (quantum, IoT, edge computing) exceeds Kogod's current offerings. However, Kogod's depth in IT governance, enterprise architecture, and IT financial management is not matched by Darden.
- **Primer implications**: Darden's concentration validates the importance of covering AI strategy, responsible AI, platform economics, and innovation management in the primer. Topics like design thinking and agile methods are also worth considering for future primer content.
