# Risk & Security

Managing technology risk is one of the most important responsibilities for modern business leaders. From unauthorized software use (shadow IT) to sophisticated cyber attacks, from data privacy regulations to analytics governance — understanding how to identify, assess, and mitigate IT risks is essential for every MBA graduate.

## Why This Matters

A single data breach can cost millions, destroy customer trust, and end careers. Regulatory non-compliance can result in massive fines. Poor data governance leads to bad decisions based on unreliable information. As a business leader, you do not need to be a security expert — but you need to understand these risks well enough to ask the right questions, set the right priorities, and make informed decisions about investment and policy.
