# Enterprise IT Fundamentals Primer for MBA Students

**AU Kogod School of Business | ITEC-617 "Information and Technology"**

---

## Welcome

This primer is your go-to reference for enterprise IT concepts that every MBA graduate needs to understand. It is designed as a companion to **ITEC-617**, a half-semester course that introduces you to information technology, analytics, and information systems in a business context.

While the course covers digital transformation, sustainability, emerging technologies, analytics, AI, change management, and technology investment through projects and case studies, it cannot dive deeply into every foundational enterprise IT topic. **This primer fills that gap** — giving you a persistent, searchable reference you can use during the course and throughout your career.

## About ITEC-617

ITEC-617 "Information and Technology" is a 1.5-credit, half-semester course required for first-year MBA students at AU Kogod. The course covers:

- Strategic and operational roles of IT in business
- Data-driven insights for operations and innovation
- Analytical tools for business decision-making
- IT resource management and governance challenges
- IT's impact on sustainability (energy, carbon footprint)
- Building business cases for technology investments

Course topics span Digital Transformation, Sustainability, Emerging Technologies, Analytics, AI & Change Management, and Technology Investment Business Cases — culminating in a "Shark Tank"-style presentation to industry judges.
